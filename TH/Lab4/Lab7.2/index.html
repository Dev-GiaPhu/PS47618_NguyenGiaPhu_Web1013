Tea Station

Once upon a time we only had a couple of teapots and enjoyed tea only with our family. Over time, we were able to afford to buy a small tea plantation.

Now we have a whole teahouse and we delight many people with delicious and hot tea. We serve black tea, green tea, jasmine tea and many other flavors.
